Guacamole

Description

A refreshing, cool classic dip made of ripe Hass avocados. Perfect for summer, or any time of the year. And heart healthy to boot!

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • ½ cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic

Steps

  1. Step 1: Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic.
  2. Step 2: Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
